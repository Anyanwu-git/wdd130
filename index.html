Nigeria

Imo State, located in southeastern Nigeria, is one of the smallest states by area but has a large population. It's known as the Eastern Heartland and is predominantly inhabited by the igbo people. The state is characterized by its diverse geography, including lowlands, oil-palm bush, and rivers like the Imo, Otamiri, and Orashi.
